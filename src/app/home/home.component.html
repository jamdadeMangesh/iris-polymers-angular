Welcome to Iris Polymers

Iris Polymers is the leading company in the industry of agricultural and packaging films

Iris Polymers Private Ltd manufactures a wide range of Mulching films like UV Stabilized Green House Covering, Low Tunnel Films, Mulch Films, Lamination Film, Shrink Films, Drainage Sheets and Pond Canal Reservoir Lining for Agriculture and Plant Protection Cover. The Company has a pan-India presence through all major states in India, Head office located at Pune. With a network of more than 1000 distributors / dealers across India and reaching out to more & more farmers. Our company a renowned leader in manufacturing and exporting a wide stock of films. The Company has presence with Italy, Spain, Ukraine & Tanzania, etc.

IRIS Polymers target customers are primary farmers. The Company keeps adding new products every year through its collaborations and is continuously on the lookout to bring the latest technology to Indian Farmers
